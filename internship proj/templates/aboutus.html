Why this website?

Most housing websites like zillow focus too much on the physical needs of the homebuyer: price range, rooms, ameneties and more. However due to Austins growing nature finding the right home can be hard, and there are many people not native to Austin coming in droves who have no idea how each area in Austin is socialy. This website focuses on the inner personal wants and need of new homebuyers to help direct them to a neighboorhood in which  that suits their lifestyle. We want homebuying to expand more than just physical limitations. Anyone can find the perfect house, the hard part is finding the right neighboorhood for you and you alone.
